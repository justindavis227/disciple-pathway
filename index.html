Southeast · Disciple-Making Pathway

Build a Disciple-Making Culturefrom the life of Christ.

A disciple-making pathway reveals where people are and where they need to go on their spiritual formation journey. A true disciple-making pathway creates a compelling picture to foster personal spiritual growth and mobilize disciple-makers.

The lost need found. The stalled need restarted. The curious need space. The believers need called. The workers need sent. The following is our simple pathway for anyone at Southeast to enter and join the adventure of becoming a disciple-maker.
